Pedgogies

This page outlines the key underpinnings of my pedagogies.

Holism

One of my central pedagogies is holism. Holism is the belief that a teacher should consider a student holistically. This includes, but is certainly not limited to, their past and current achievements, their interests, their home life, and maybe even how many pets they have. All learners are different, and the ways that we prefer to learn also differ and so it is important that educators consider the unique ways that each and every student learns to uplift class and individual achievement.

You could read more about holoism here.

The TPACK model

The TPACK model outlines that technology and education have clear overlaps and that the two can mutually foster and benefit the other. My background with computational mathematics has given me the procedural fluency with computers and technology to integrate these into my classroom. The benefits of this include streamlining differentiation and adjustments and less overall administration time which means I can focus on my craft as an educator.
